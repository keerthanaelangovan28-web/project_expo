PROJECT EXPO

Where Innovation Meets Creativity.

The Project Expo is a platform for students to showcase their technical projects and working prototypes across domains such as software, hardware, AI, and emerging technologies. Open to all departments, the expo encourages participants to present ideas, demonstrate functionality, and share solutions that address real-world challenges.

Build. Exhibit. Inspire.

    TEAM FORMATION GUIDELINES

    Choose your track — compete within your department or collaborate across boundaries

    🏢

    Intra-Department Teams

    All members from SAME department
    • All team members must be from the SAME department
    • Team Leader will be the primary point of contact
    • Compete for "Department Awards" (Champion, Runner Up, 2nd Runner Up)
    • Showcase your specialized domain expertise
    ✓ Register under Department Awards category
    🔗

    Inter-Department Teams

    Tech Fusion Track
    • Team members must be from DIFFERENT departments
    • Team Leader will be the primary point of contact
    • Compete for "Tech Fusion Awards" (Champion, Runner Up, 2nd Runner Up)
    • Demonstrate interdisciplinary collaboration
    ✓ Register under Tech Fusion Awards category
    ⚡

    IMPORTANT PARTICIPATION RULE

    Each student can participate in MAXIMUM TWO teams:

    1 ONE Intra-Department team
    2 ONE Inter-Department team

    ❌ Students CANNOT join multiple teams within the same category

    ❌ Students CANNOT join two Intra-Department teams

    ❌ Students CANNOT join two Inter-Department teams

    📋

    General Requirements

    • Each team must have a Team Leader
    • Team Leader will be the primary point of contact
    • Maximum 5 students per team
    • Teams must declare their category (Intra OR Tech Fusion) during registration
    • Teams cannot change category after registration deadline
    • All team members must be currently enrolled students
