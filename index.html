PROJECT EXPO

Where Innovation Meets Creativity.

The Project Expo is a platform for students to showcase their technical projects and working prototypes across domains such as software, hardware, AI, and emerging technologies. Open to all departments, the expo encourages participants to present ideas, demonstrate functionality, and share solutions that address real-world challenges.
